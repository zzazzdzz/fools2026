Balance changes

Several changes to moves, items and game mechanics have been implemented for a more pleasant experience. Below is a detailed list of all changes. If you're an experienced player, it is recommended that you familiarize yourself with the changelog, to avoid surprises and prepare new strategies.

Snatch Orb
Identify Orb
  • Removed from the game.
One-Shot Orb
Two-Edge Orb
  • The effects now have 100% base accuracy.
Possess Orb
  • Unused item, reintroduced to the game.
  • A held item which allows the leader to survive KO by possessing a nearby Pokémon.
  • Now able to possess enemies.
  • Buy price increased to 2000, sale price increased to 800.
Hurl Orb
  • Collisions now deal 60 fixed damage instead of 5.
Fill-In Orb
  • Works on all secondary terrain (not just water and magma).
Rebound Orb
  • Now applies Counter instead of Mini-Counter.
Silence Orb
  • Muzzled status reworked to Silenced.
  • A silenced enemy is unable to use any moves, including regular attacks.
Gambler Orb
  • Newly added item.
  • Upon usage, the contents of the leader's Toolbox are randomized.
  • Ignores floor enchantment effects, whatever that could mean.
  • 90% of gamblers quit before they win big.
Evolve Orb
  • Newly added item.
  • Causes the leader to evolve upon entering the next floor, as long as level requirements are met.
  • Skitty evolves at level 20. No item is required.
Blessed Orb
  • Newly added item.
  • Removes all negative enchantments on next floor and replaces them with positive enchantments, whatever that could mean.
Sonicboom
  • Fixed damage reduced from 55 to 30.
Vacuum-Cut
  • Now deals base damage equal to user's level.
  • Base PP raised to 10.
  • Vacuum-Cut TMs can be found in dungeons and learned by any Pokémon.
Excavate
  • Unused move, reintroduced to the game.
  • Digs the wall in front of the user.
  • Excavate TMs can be found in dungeons and learned by any Pokémon.
Spin Slash
  • Unused move, reintroduced to the game.
  • Deals typeless damage in a one-tile radius around the user.
  • Spin Slash TMs can be found in dungeons and learned by any Pokémon.
Pitfall Trap
  • Now drops the leader to the previous floor instead of the next floor.
Ginseng
  • Each instance of Ginseng usage increases the move's base power by 6 (previously 1).
Joy Ribbon
  • Grants experience equal to 15 times the damage received (previously 1x).
Heal Ribbon
  • Now heals additional 1 HP per turn in addition to other effects.
  • Ignores floor enchantment effects, whatever that could mean.
Serene Ribbon
  • Newly added item.
  • Prevents floor enchantments from affecting the player, whatever that could mean.
Warp Scarf
  • Holding the item causes the leader's Belly to empty twice as fast, similar to Munch Belt.
  • Effects unchanged when held by other Pokémon.
Pure Seed
  • Newly added item.
  • Warps Pokémon directly to stairway.
  • If the stairway is locked, using a Pure Seed unlocks it - whatever that could mean.
Reviver Seed
  • Buy price increased to 2000, sale price increased to 800.
Life Seed
  • Now grants +5 max HP (was 3).
Sitrus Berry
  • Now grants +3 max HP (was 2).
System changes
  • Items and money are not lost upon defeat.
  • Quicksaving is not available - instead, progress is saved automatically at floors 11, 21, 31, 51, 71 and 100.
  • Certain items (Apple, Max Elixer) are guaranteed to appear on certain floors.
  • Experience formula was changed to account for shorter playtime.
  • All Pokémon now yield a minimum of 40 base Experience.
  • Sticky throwables no longer spawn on dungeon floors.
  • Several new mechanics have been added. It's a secret. Go play the game!
